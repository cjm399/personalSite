Code Tycoon - 2021

Built for Okta, Code Tycoon is a WebGL game built in Unity where you are a programmer at a failing company.


I worked on a majority of the online functionality, integrated PlayFab, implemented Cloud Saving, utilized Okta for user authentication. I also worked on many of the game play systems, mini-games, character creator, movement, and animation systems.

Resilience - 2020

“Best Student Game” Games For Change 2020


This is an intergalactic first-person city-builder, in which you manage a refugee camp. Balancing refugee stats to ensure they are healthy and happy, while trying to resettle refugees onto foreign planets makes up the primary game play.


For my senior project, I am serving on a team of 18 members to deliver a game that addresses issues in refugee aid organizations. The goal of this game is to encourage players to learn more about the refugee crisis outside of the game.

Cryptid Hunter - 2019


Cryptids, also known as folk lore creatures, are real, and you try to prove it by finding them and taking their pictures. You are dropped into a camp, with a camera, and are tasked with finding the Fresno Night Walker. Once found, get a good enough picture of the cryptid to prove that Night Walker’s exist, and return to camp.


This first person photography game, was created by a team of 7 students across an 8 week development period.

Game Jams


An Artist’s Dream - 2018


An Artist’s Dream is an experimental hybrid art project made within 1 week. It is playable for free on itch.io in browser. The art was created by Kelly and the rest was created by myself.


Orbit Toy - 2020


A “toy” (meaning that it isn’t a game but a single piece or mechanic that could exist within a game, no objective given) that I made in about an hour for a game jam in which we had to make a “toy” with the theme “revolution.”


In this toy, you are placing planets and seeing how all of the celestial bodies act with one another and you can try and get a planet of yours into orbit.


Space Hat - 2018


An infinite game I made in a couple hours for a game jam. You use the arrow sticks to fly the hat around, it rotates with physics as you fly towards some cool stars with a trail particle effect.


StarField 476 - 2018


This experimental game project was created in a day. The assignment was to create a game that is reflective of the games industry today. I chose to focus on the AAA games that are tending to release incomplete games, that patch everything together after the fact or through the use of paid DLC.
