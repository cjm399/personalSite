The Mighty Bananas

My Final for my Animation 211 class at Drexel. We were tasked with recreating a 30+ second clip from a live action film/video. I recreated the car chase scene from a TV show called The Mighty Boosh, episode, The Nightmare Of Milky Joe.


Old Golf

Week 5 of my animation class at Drexel. Anim 211. Assignment: Given this modeled, UVed, and Rigged Sack, texture and animate it and make them overcome some kind of obstetrical in a 10 - 20 second video. I decided to make this sack an Old Man walking on the sidewalk, when they come across a rock blocking the road.


The Great Race!

Week 4 of my animation class at Drexel. Anim 211. Assignment: Create a 15 second video in which 2-3 geometric objects race against one another. With a clear goal, and it is hard to tell who is going to win. For this I decided on a scene in which a pencil and pen are racing to get into the cup.


Adventure Of The Ring

Week 3 of my animation class at Drexel. Anim 211. Assignment: Create a 15 second video in which something was going on an adventure in which you needed to move the camera. For this, I decided to have ring travel through a restaurant, out into the world.


Bouncing Balls

Week 2 of my animation class at Drexel. Anim 211. Assignment: Create a 10 second video in which 3 balls of different physical properties are bouncing. For this I decided on a tennis ball, basket ball, and cue ball.


Whack-A-Mole

Week 1 of my animation class at Drexel. Anim 211. Assignment: Create a 10 second looping video in which the looping point is disguised. 5 different objects must be animated. For this I decided on a whack a mole scene.
